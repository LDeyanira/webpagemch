About us

Our virtual reality club is a dynamic hub where students collaborate on projects spanning diverse fields such as chemistry, mechatronics, electronics, and medicine. With a focus on enhancing people's quality of life, our projects aim to simplify tasks and even enhance existing processes. Through the innovative use of VR technology, we strive to create solutions that are accessible and impactful. Join us in exploring the endless possibilities of virtual reality for the betterment of society.
