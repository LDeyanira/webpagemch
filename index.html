Resume

Doctor in Materials Sciences from the Academic Area of ​​Materials Sciences Earth and Materials from the Autonomous University of the State of Hidalgo (UAEH). Master in Chemical Sciences with Honorable Mention from the Faculty of Chemistry from the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), with a specialty in Heterogeneous Catalysis and Methods of Synthesis and Characterization of Nanomaterials and Lic. in Chemistry from the Autonomous University of the State of Hidalgo with Recognition and Medal for Academic Merit with a specialty in Chemistry Analytics. Doctor Macaria has published JCR articles, articles published in magazines indexed by CONACyT and popular articles. He has directed and participated in research projects within the IPN, collaborated in projects with the IMP, UNAM and the UAEH, has graduated undergraduate students of the National Polytechnic Institute, is a member of the National System of C level researchers and full-time professor in the Professional Unit Interdisciplinary Engineering Campus Hidalgo of the National Polytechnic Institute (UPIIH-IPN)
