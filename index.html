Full-Time Proffesor-Researcher, Level in the SNI:C UPIIH - Instituto Politecnico Nacional.

Why did you decide to apply for Mentoring in Science Program?

In the STEM areas the lag of women is very evident, I'm working in a engineering school and this sadly occurs. I can make a difference by being trained to work width female students seeking to excel in areas of engineering and basic sciences as their mentor. As a stdudent, I did not have this support and it would definetily have made a difference to have such support in all areas, it would have saved me time when making important decisions about my professional training

What benefits did you receive through this program as a mentor?

The opportunity to be trainer as a Science Mentor by Innova Consultancy with the support and funding of the British Council allowed me to expand my networks and start projects with very successful women sho participated in this program. We build friendships and support networks, share academic and proffesional information, and look at how to share this information with our members and students

From your point of view, how does this programme benefit the early career researchers being mentored?

It help young proffesionals exploit their abilities to visualize and apply different methodologies that allow them to achieve their personal and professional goals Sometimes, we just need a little puch and have self-confidence to achieve it, organize ourselves bether and get down to work with small objectives that are being achieved by little.
